Travelling

Another favorite hobby of mine is travelling. I really like all kinds of travel: by car, by train, by plane, and travelling on foot. Sadly, I have never travelled by a ship yet 😪. Usually, I prefer travelling with my friends but sometimes I prefer travelling by myself. The reasons I find travelling so interesting are: seeing new and beautiful sights, exploring different cultures and meeting lots of new people. My latest journey was in Georgia in August 2019. The trip was divided into 3 days in Tbilisi (Georgia's capital city) and 4 days in hiking the Mestia Ushguli mountains.

Tbilisi

Tbilisi is very beautiful and romantic city that has a lot to offer. The places I liked and recommend to see are Narikala-Fortress and The Bridge of peace. These two places are pretty close by and near them you can find a cable that has a beautiful view to the whole city. Tbilisi is very cheap, so shopping there is really great, the Tbilisi mall is a great place for it. This is me in The Bridge of peace:

P.S if you see the owner of the monkey and parrot, know that he'll just throw them on you and actually charge you for that 😓.

Mestia Ushguli Mountains

There are 2 ways to get from Tbilisi to the Mestia Ushguli Mountains: The easy way is by a train that leaves Tbilisi two times a day: one in the afternoon and one in the night. The harder way is to get there by a place. I said harder since this flight from Tbilisi is pretty rare and you need to book it about 5 months in advance. This is me and few friends in our way there:

Mestia Ushguli Mountains is the main reason I chose to visit Tbilisi. This place posses a very unique tracking hike that is 4 days long and is suitable also for beginners.
